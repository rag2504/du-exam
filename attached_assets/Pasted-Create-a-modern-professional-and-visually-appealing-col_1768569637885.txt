Create a modern, professional, and visually appealing college website for "Darshan University" that demonstrates their Digital Answer Paper Evaluation System.

Purpose:
The website should clearly show that Darshan University evaluates exam papers digitally through a multi-level verification process after scanning handwritten answer sheets.

Branding:
- College Name: Darshan University
- Use official-style academic colors (blue, white, light gray)
- Clean, trustworthy, and technology-driven look
- Use smooth animations and professional icons
- Responsive for mobile, tablet, and desktop

Website Structure:

1. Hero Section:
- Large heading: "Digital Answer Paper Evaluation System"
- Subheading: "Ensuring Accuracy, Transparency & Quality in Examination Assessment"
- CTA Buttons:
  - "How It Works"
  - "Explore the Process"
- Background: Abstract tech/education-themed design

2. About Digital Evaluation:
- Explain that handwritten answer papers are scanned securely
- Evaluations are done digitally to improve efficiency and accuracy
- Mention alignment with modern academic standards
- Keep the language formal and institutional

3. Digital Evaluation Workflow (Core Section):
Show the process visually using cards, steps, or timeline:

Step 1: Answer Paper Scanning
- Secure scanning of handwritten answer sheets
- Encrypted digital storage

Step 2: Level 1 Evaluation – Junior Supervisor
- Initial evaluation and marking
- Focus on correctness and completeness

Step 3: Level 2 Evaluation – Senior Supervisor
- Review of marks and evaluation quality
- Ensures academic consistency

Step 4: Level 3 Evaluation – University Authority
- Final verification by senior academic staff
- Approval and result finalization

4. Key Benefits Section:
- High Accuracy & Transparency
- Reduced Human Error
- Faster Result Processing
- Secure Digital Records
- Multi-Level Quality Checks

5. Technology & Security:
- Secure digital access
- Role-based evaluation system
- Audit trails for transparency
- Data protection and confidentiality

6. FAQs Section:
Include accordion-style FAQs such as:
- What is digital evaluation?
- How are handwritten papers checked digitally?
- Is the evaluation secure?
- How many levels of checking are involved?
- Does digital evaluation reduce errors?
- Who has access to the answer papers?

7. University Trust Section:
- Highlight Darshan University’s commitment to innovation
- Emphasize academic integrity and excellence
- Optional stats or highlights (years of excellence, students, programs)

8. Footer:
- Darshan University name
- Links: Home | Process | FAQs | Contact
- Copyright © Darshan University
- Clean academic footer design

Technical Requirements:
- Use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript only
- No backend required
- Smooth scrolling
- Subtle animations on scroll
- Modern UI components
- Well-structured, commented code
- Keep all content professional and suitable for a university presentation

Make the website look impressive enough for:
- College project submission
- Internal university demo
- Presentation to faculty members
